This photo series began from my desire of enligting chinese women and to get closer from my origins. I'm a child of immigrants, I was born and raised in Paris by my mom who is colombian. My biological father is sino american. I'v never been able to be in contact with my Chinese roots.

Growing up looking as a half Chinese woman in a predominantly white society that still holds habits of ordinary and systemic racism was very ambiguous, for a long time french racism was the only mirror to my origins. Those last years I was able to create and photograph more and more asian women which allowed me to have a clearer view of myself and the position of persons of color in our societies
